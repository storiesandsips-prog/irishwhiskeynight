What is Irish Whiskey Night?

A shared whiskey experience,
one Thursday at a time.

Once a month, people across Ireland — and around the world — sit down to taste Irish whiskey together.

Three drams. The makers behind them. One live experience.

Join with this month's tasting kit, or tune in free and be part of the night.
